A property card represents ownership of a percentage share in a particular property. 

These property cards will entitle the holder to regular rental income which is paid proportionally based on the value of cards owned.

For example, if a property generates £2500 per month in rent and you own property cards worth 2% of the property, you would receive an annual income of £600 just for holding the card.

Real estate has always been the investment of choice for the wealthy for a number of reasons.

Stability - The real estate market in the UK has historically shown resilience, offering stability in uncertain times. House prices double on average every 8.7 years.

Demand - There's a persistent demand for housing in the UK due to factors like population growth, urbanization, and a shortage of affordable housing. This demand provides a solid foundation for property investment.

Diversification - Real estate tends to have a low correlation with other asset classes like stocks and bonds, meaning it can help spread risk.

Affordability - With the fractionized ownership offered my MyProperTree you can begin your investment journey from as little as £100, unlocking opportunities previously reserved for those with large amounts of capital.

Simplicity - The knowledge required and time involved in real estate investments can be daunting. MyProperTree handle the acquisitions, maintenance and paperwork for all properties making the process for our users smooth and simple.
